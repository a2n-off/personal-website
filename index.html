#1 Funepro [SME, funerary art, turnover 12M€]

The aim of this mission was to migrate their old back-office, written in Flash, to Angular. We also migrated their entire IS to the cloud.

  • Managed the migration of their back-office from Flash to Angular, saving 2 hours on each express order shipment
  • Supervised the migration of their infrastructure to the cloud, ensuring partner interconnections, saving 98k€/year by eliminating site downtime.

#2 Mooncard [Scaleup, +75.7M$ in series A to C]

The main objective of this mission was to create an API to integrate VINCI's freeway badge system into their payment system. This API was to act as a "bridge" between Mooncard and VINCI, processing all information received by VINCI and then transmitting it to Mooncard as expected.

  • Managed 4 developers for the creation of an API & APP to integrate Vinci highway badges into their payment system.
  • Coordinated topics between Vinci, Mooncard, and Helyx teams, ensuring on-time and on-budget deliveries.
  • duration: 8 months
  • team: 4 devs
  • org: agile, scrumban
